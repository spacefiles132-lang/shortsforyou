Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

ShortsForYou ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ShortsForYou application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Credentials: TikTok OAuth tokens used to post content on your behalf. These are stored securely and encrypted.
  • Video Content: Videos you upload through our service are transmitted directly to TikTok's servers. We do not store your video content on our own servers.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics including publication timestamps, post status, and error logs to diagnose issues.

How We Use Your Information

  • To post video content to TikTok on your behalf and at your direction
  • To notify you of publication status (success/failure)
  • To improve and maintain the service

Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and are never shared with third parties. Video content is transmitted directly to TikTok and is not retained on our servers after successful upload.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your TikTok OAuth tokens are stored securely and are only used for the purpose of posting content as directed by you.

Your Rights

You may revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok app settings. Contact us to request deletion of any stored credentials.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the developer email associated with your ShortsForYou account.
